Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
  3. Mix in melted butter, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add sugar, egg, and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  5. Stir in the flour until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 60-65 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Allow to cool before slicing and serving.
